# Network Change Review Checklist

## Change Quality

- Objective is clear and measurable.
- Affected devices and interfaces are listed.
- Commands are reviewed before execution.
- Rollback steps are documented.
- Verification commands are included.

## Risk Review

- Management access impact is understood.
- Redundant path impact is understood.
- Maintenance window is defined.
- Monitoring and alert expectations are clear.

## Evidence

- Pre-check output captured.
- Post-check output captured.
- Final status recorded.
- Follow-up actions assigned.
